About the Color Classifier

The Color Classifier game teaches the basics of supervised learning by letting students label randomly generated colors as “Warm” or “Cool,” train a simple k-Nearest Neighbors model, and then test its predictions on new colors.

How to Play

  • Click New Color to generate a random RGB color.
  • Select Warm or Cool from the dropdown.
  • Click Add Example to save your label.
  • After several examples, click Train to build the model.
  • Finally, click Predict to see whether the model classifies the current color as Warm or Cool.
